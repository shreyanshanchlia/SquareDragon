Energy To Control

Energy to Control is a GMTK Game Jam submission. Move the character by pressing "SPACE" which launches the character away from energy zones. You must get to the next energy zone, so launch carefully. The theme for GMTK Game Jam was "out of control" as the player can only be controlled inside energy zones, making them "out of control" when not in these zones.
